Our Story

Meet Sagar, Our Founder

"Technology is not just about devices; it's about solving real problems."

Founded in 2014 by Sagar Patel, DOT Technologie began as a small computer repair shop in Bhayandar with a vision to provide reliable and affordable tech solutions to the local community.

With over 8 years of experience in computer hardware and network solutions, Sagar built DOT Technologie from the ground up, starting with basic laptop repairs and gradually expanding to offer comprehensive IT services.

What started as a one-person operation has now grown into a trusted technology hub serving hundreds of satisfied customers. Our commitment to quality service and customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do.

Our Mission

To provide cutting-edge technology solutions with personalized service, ensuring our customers can rely on their devices for both work and leisure without interruptions.
